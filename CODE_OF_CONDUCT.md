# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Cinekive a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity, experience level, education, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Focusing on what is best for filmmakers and local-first tools
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when mistakes happen
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal/political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Encouraging copyright infringement or redistributing still libraries you do not have rights to
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Report issues to **hello@gianlucaimprota.com**. Maintainers will review and respond as appropriate. We may remove comments, lock threads, or ban accounts that violate this Code.
